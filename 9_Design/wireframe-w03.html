Module 1 > Identify Azure AI services

Azure AI services are cloud-based APIs that enable applications to see, hear, speak, understand language, and make decisions. They require no deep ML expertise — developers can integrate AI capabilities with a few lines of code.

Key service categories include:

  • Vision — image classification, object detection, OCR, facial recognition
  • Speech — speech-to-text, text-to-speech, translation, speaker recognition
  • Language — sentiment analysis, key phrase extraction, language detection, QnA
  • Decision — anomaly detection, content moderation, personalizer
Source: Microsoft Learn — AI-901 Module 1
