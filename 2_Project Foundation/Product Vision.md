## Product Vision Statement

> To provide certification exam candidates with an AI-powered learning coach built on the official syllabus as its knowledge map, ensuring that both learning content and assessment methods are precisely aligned with the exam syllabus in scope, structure, terminology, and depth. Candidates can retrieve any required knowledge and receive targeted practice exercises in the fewest possible steps at any moment, delivering a **precise, reliable, and convenient** learning experience.

## Product Vision Board

### Target Group
- Microsoft AI-901 certification exam candidates
- Future: candidates of other certification exams

### User Needs
- Learning content precisely aligned with the official syllabus
- Fast retrieval of fuzzy concepts
- Verifiable learning outcomes

### Product
- Not another general-purpose AI learning tool
- A **digital learning coach** purpose-built for certification exams
- Core capabilities: syllabus-based knowledge map + on-demand knowledge retrieval + targeted practice

### Business Goals
- Solve the core contradiction of AI tools in certification preparation
- Build a replicable certification learning platform model
- First validation: Microsoft AI-901

## Keywords

**Precise · Reliable · Convenient**
