## Market Gap

There is a clear gap in the current market for certification exam preparation:

| Tool Type | Example | Strength | Gap in Certification Prep |
|-----------|---------|----------|--------------------------|
| General-purpose AI Chat | ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini | Broad knowledge, fluent responses | Does not follow syllabus; scope and depth uncontrollable |
| Official Learning Platform | Microsoft Learn | Authoritative, exam-aligned | Knowledge scattered across pages; inconvenient retrieval |
| AI Note-taking Tool | NotebookLM | Source-based Q&A | Uncontrollable source selection; inaccurate structure parsing |
| Traditional Exam Dumps | ExamTopics, MeasureUp | Exam-like questions | Practice only; no systematic learning capability |

## The Opportunity

> **Build a certification learning platform powered by AI, with the official syllabus as its knowledge backbone — bridging the gap between "general-purpose AI tools" and "official learning materials."**

What makes this opportunity unique:

1. **Not replacing official materials** — using the official syllabus as the single source of truth, with better organization and delivery
2. **Not general-purpose AI** — focused exclusively on certification preparation, done deep and well
3. **Not just a question bank** — covering the full learning cycle: study, practice, and retrieval

## Why Now

| Factor | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| AI Maturity | LLMs are now capable of structured knowledge teaching; the challenge is control, not capability |
| User Behavior Established | Candidates already use AI for exam prep — the demand is validated |
| Market Educated | Users have experienced the limitations of general-purpose tools and are receptive to vertical solutions |
| Certification Demand Growing | Microsoft AI-901 and similar AI certifications are in increasing demand |

## Product Positioning

A **vertical learning tool** focused on the certification preparation scenario — not a general-purpose AI platform.

💡 **Sidenote — B2B expansion possibility (unvalidated):** The core capability of "syllabus-scoped AI learning" may also apply in educational institutions where teachers want to control the scope of AI-assisted learning within their course curriculum. This is noted as a potential future direction but is not part of the current project scope.

## Core Assumptions (To Be Validated)

> - Candidates are willing to switch to a new tool that ensures "alignment with the official syllabus"
> - Syllabus-as-knowledge-map interaction provides a better learning experience than general-purpose AI chat
> - Certification training providers / enterprises would invest resources in such a platform
